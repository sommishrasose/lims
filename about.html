Welcome to Labmitra

At LIMS-MITRA, we are dedicated to revolutionizing laboratory operations through state-of-the-art technology that not only streamlines processes but also enhances productivity and accuracy. With a decade of experience behind us, our mission is to lead innovation and automation in healthcare through our comprehensive lab software solutions.

We offer a fully automated laboratory system, complete with barcoding and analyzer interface solutions for a wide range of analyzers. Our system supports both unidirectional and bidirectional communication, significantly reducing errors, automating processes, and minimizing the time required for reporting. This level of automation ensures that laboratories can operate more efficiently and deliver results faster.

Designed with user-friendliness in mind, our interface is simple yet powerful, ensuring ease of operation without compromising on functionality. Our system includes advanced features such as account management for all clients, tracking credit and debit transactions, patient receipts, and checking outstanding balances. Additionally, in cases of balance due, we offer reporting print-blocking facilities to manage financial operations effectively.

At LIMS-MITRA, we pride ourselves on offering a wide array of features that cater to the diverse needs of modern laboratories. While our solutions are comprehensive, we remain flexible and open to customizing the software based on specific customer requirements, ensuring that our clients always have the tools they need to succeed.
