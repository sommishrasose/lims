LIMS-MITRA

Laboratory Information & Management System

At LIMS-MITRA, we are committed to revolutionizing laboratory management with our advanced software solutions. Our platform is designed to automate and optimize laboratory workflows, ensuring greater accuracy, efficiency, and security. Our comprehensive system features robust data management tools, a sophisticated barcoding system, and seamless analyzer interface solutions.


Our barcoding system and analyzer interface solutions—both uni directional and bi-directional significantly reduce errors, automate routine tasks, and accelerate reporting times. The integration of our software with a wide range of analyzers helps streamline sample tracking and data collection. Additionally, our full-featured web application allows online access, making it easy for users to manage and review reports from anywhere.

Key features include QR code functionality for direct report downloads, integrated SMS APIs for sending report links to patients and other stakeholders, and a user-friendly interface that simplifies operations. We offer customizable solutions to meet specific needs, including account management, patient receipt tracking, and balance reporting. With LIMS-MITRA, you can be assured of a solution that adapts to your laboratory's evolving requirements.

Modules Features

LIMS-MITRA Laboratory Management System

1

Scanning & Uploading of Documents

LIMS-MITRA enables seamless scanning and uploading of documents, making clinical records easily accessible and well-organized. This feature supports efficient record-keeping and quick retrieval of patient information.

2

Barcode Management

Our barcode management system enhances sample tracking accuracy and efficiency. With robust barcoding features, LIMS-MITRA ensures precise labeling, reduces manual errors, and speeds up lab processes through automated tracking.

3

Billing and Insurance

LIMS-MITRA simplifies billing and insurance workflows with comprehensive management tools. Our system offers organized billing processes, detailed financial reporting, and insurance claim tracking, streamlining your financial operations.

4

Multi-User & Multi-Site Compatibility

Designed for flexibility, LIMS-MITRA supports multiple users across various sites. This multi-site compatibility ensures seamless integration, efficient collaboration, and consistent data access regardless of location.

5

Browser Independent (IE/Mozilla/Chrome)

LIMS-MITRA is compatible with all major web browsers, including Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, and Google Chrome. This browser independence provides users with the flexibility to access the system from any preferred browser.

6

Data Structure

Our data structure feature allows for the classification of values based on age and gender, marking them as normal, abnormal, or critical. This functionality ensures that data is accurately categorized and easily interpreted for better clinical decision-making.
